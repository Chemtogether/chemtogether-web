Dear members of the Department

Next week, the career fair Chemtogether takes place, to which you are cordially invited.

On Tuesday and Wednesday, November 5th and 6th, a total of 18 companies from the chemical and pharmaceutical industries will be present. In addition to interesting conversations with company representatives, there will be coffee and Gipfeli for free and a supporting program consisting of two pre-events, CV picture photoshoots, CV checks and company flash presentations. Each day will end with an apro at around 4:00 pm.

Please find the full program below.

Pre-Events

Thursday, October 31st, 6pm in HIL E4

ETH Career Center: Live Interview
Job interviews make you nervous? You dont know what job is a great fit for your CV? Then join us for the Interviewtraining with the ETH Career Center and get some answers. During the Interviewtraining with the ETH Career Center, a current D-CHAB student will be interviewed live by the experts of the Career Center. It will also be explained why he or she is a great fit for that job. The interview is followed by a discussion of the performance and tips & tricks for job interviews.
Apro afterwards.

 

Monday, November 4th, 6pm in HCI G3

Panel Discussion: The Future of Chemistry in a Digital World
What is the outlook for Chemists in the context of the Fourth Industrial Revolution and the changing nature of work? How are these changes impacting the skills and knowledge the chemists of tomorrow need to be equipped with today?
Panel discussion with these distinguished guests:

  • Dr. Margherita Fontana - DOW, Commercial Director Construction Chemicals
  • Dr. Stefan Hildbrand - Roche, Head of Process Chemistry & Catalysis Small Molecules Technical Development
  • Prof. Dr. Jonathan Hall - ETH Zrich, Professor for Pharmaceutical Chemistry

Moderation by Viktoria Ivarsson, International Relations Officer at ETH Zrich.
Apro afterwards.
